The target face will rotate after the command is received, EXCEPT for tilt command.
For left, right, pan calibraion, you just need to send the command and wait.
For tilt calibration, three different commands need to be sended. And you need move the face manually.

left face auto calibraion: 50
right face auto calibration: 51
pan auto calibration: 52
tilt calibration at -90 degree: 53
tilt calibration at 0 degree: 54
tilt calibration at 90 degree: 55
